Finding the highest pitch

Starting from C4, you will hear the sound of each note until C6. Once you click start, try to sing along, with "Ah"s or "Hm"s and match the detected note with the played note as much as possible.


The teal coloured indicator is the pitch which is being tested. The purple coloured indicator is the pitch which you are singing.
